Welcome to News-Link

Your News Article Recommender

News-link is a reccomendation engine for journalists working for digital news publishers. Using NLP, News-Link provides journalists with a list previously published similar news stories. Links to these stories can be easily inserted into a story that a journalist is currently writing, to provide readers with additional, related news content.

This build of New-Link compares your story to a databse of >1500 news articles scraped from the CBC.ca.

Interested in learning more about News-Link's backend? Check out this GitHub Repository.
