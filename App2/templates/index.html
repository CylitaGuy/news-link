What is News-Link Anyway?

News-link is a reccomendation engine for journalists working at large digital news publishers. Using NLP, News-Link provides journalits with a list previously published similar news stories. Links to these stories can then be easily inserted into a story that a journalist is currently writing, to provide their readers with additional, related news content.

This build of New-Link compares your story of interest to a databse of >1500 news articles scraped from the CBC.ca.

Interested in learning more about News-Link's backend? Check out its GitHub Repository.
